# Character Library

Characters are reusable across every simulation.

Every Character has

- Name
- Color
- Optional Icon
- One unique Skill implementation per simulation

Characters never change color.

---

## Heavy

Color Expansion

- Capture two cells.

Weapon Clash

- Gain damage after every hit.

---

## Swift

Color Expansion

- Faster movement.

Weapon Clash

- Faster weapon rotation after every hit.

---

## Sleeper

Color Expansion

- Sleep, then rush.

Weapon Clash

- Weapon sleeps, then spins rapidly.

---

## Trickster

Color Expansion

- Random movement bonuses.

Weapon Clash

- Random stat upgrades.
